How to Check Your Public Gold Account Balance — Step-by-Step Guide

Quick Answer

Log in to your Public Gold account at publicgold.com.my, then navigate to your GAP 24K account from the member dashboard. You will see your total gold balance in grams, your transaction history, and the current estimated value of your holdings. To check the live gold price separately, visit PublicGoldPrice.com anytime without logging in.

Once you have registered as a Public Gold member and made your first purchase, you will want to check in on your gold balance regularly. If you are new to the platform, navigating the member account area can be a little unfamiliar at first. This guide walks you through everything — from logging in to understanding what your balance actually shows.

What You Can Check in Your Public Gold Account

Your Public Gold member account gives you access to several important pieces of information:

  • Gold balance in grams — your total accumulated GAP 24K gold, shown in grams to several decimal places
  • Current estimated value in RM — your gram balance multiplied by the current live buyback price
  • Transaction history — a full record of every purchase you have made, including date, amount in grams, and price paid per gram
  • Account statement — downloadable record of your transactions for your personal records or tax purposes
  • Personal details and bank account — your registered information that you can update if needed

How to Log In to Your Public Gold Account

  1. Go to publicgold.com.my — open any browser and navigate to the official Public Gold website.
  2. Click "Login" or "Member Login" — look for the login button at the top of the page.
  3. Enter your username and password — these were set when you registered your account. Your username is typically your registered email address or a username you chose during sign-up.
  4. Complete any security verification — Public Gold may require a one-time password (OTP) sent to your registered mobile number for security.
  5. You are now in your member dashboard — from here you can access all your account information.

If you have forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the steps to reset it via your registered email address.

How to Check Your GAP 24K Gold Balance

  1. From the member dashboard, navigate to your accounts — look for a section labelled "My Accounts", "GAP Account", or "Gold Accumulation Programme".
  2. Select your GAP 24K account — if you have multiple accounts (e.g. both GAP 24K and SAP 999 silver), select the gold one.
  3. View your balance summary — you will see your total grams accumulated, and the current estimated RM value based on the live market price at the time of viewing.
  4. View transaction history — scroll down or click the history tab to see a breakdown of every purchase: date, grams acquired, price per gram paid, and total amount.

Understanding Your Balance — What the Numbers Mean

New investors sometimes get confused by what the balance shows. Here is a clear explanation of each figure:

  • Total grams (e.g. 8.632g) — the actual physical gold equivalent you own, stored and insured by Public Gold
  • Current value (e.g. RM3,720) — your grams × the current live buyback price. This number changes every time the gold price moves. It is the approximate amount you would receive if you sold everything today.
  • Total invested (e.g. RM3,600) — the total ringgit you have paid in across all your purchases. Compare this to the current value to see your unrealised gain or loss.
  • Average cost per gram — some views show this as total invested ÷ total grams. This is your personal average purchase price, useful for evaluating your DCA performance.

To understand the current live gold price and how your balance value is calculated, check PublicGoldPrice.com which shows the latest Public Gold buy and sell prices updated every 3 minutes.

How to Download Your Account Statement

Public Gold allows you to download a record of your transactions. This is useful for your own financial tracking, and may be required for tax declaration purposes depending on your situation.

  1. Log in to your Public Gold account.
  2. Navigate to your GAP 24K account and find the transaction history or statement section.
  3. Select the date range you want to export.
  4. Download the statement in the available format (PDF or CSV).

Checking Gold Price Without Logging In

If you just want to check the current Public Gold price — without going through the login process — you can do this at PublicGoldPrice.com at any time. The tracker shows the live GAP 24K buy and sell price, updated every 3 minutes, along with a 7-day price history. No account or login required.

This is particularly useful for quickly estimating the current value of your holdings. Simply take your gram balance from your account and multiply by the current buyback price shown on PublicGoldPrice.com.

Common Account Issues and Solutions

  • Forgot password — use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset via your registered email.
  • OTP not received — check that your registered mobile number is correct. Contact Public Gold customer service if the issue persists.
  • Balance not updating after a purchase — allow a few minutes for the transaction to be processed and reflected. If it still does not appear after 24 hours, contact Public Gold support with your payment receipt.
  • Incorrect personal details — update your profile information through the account settings section before making any transactions.
Key Takeaways

  • Your balance shows grams accumulated, current RM value, and total amount invested
  • Current value = your grams × live buyback price, which changes with the market
  • Download transaction statements from your account history for your financial records
  • Check the live gold price anytime without logging in at PublicGoldPrice.com
  • For password issues, use the Forgot Password link on the login page
