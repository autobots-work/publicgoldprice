What is Public Gold GAP 24K? Complete Beginner's Guide

Quick Answer

Public Gold GAP 24K, or the Gold Accumulation Program, is a Shariah-compliant gold investment program by Public Gold Malaysia that lets investors buy and accumulate 24-karat pure gold gradually. Prices are updated in real-time with global gold market movements, and gold can be redeemed in physical form (bars, coins) or sold back at any time.

What Does GAP 24K Stand For?

GAP 24K stands for Gold Accumulation Program, 24-Karat. It is a structured gold investment product offered by Public Gold Marketing Sdn. Bhd., one of Malaysia's largest and most trusted gold investment companies.

The "24K" refers to 24-karat gold — the purest form of gold available, at 99.9% purity. This is the same gold used in investment-grade gold bars and coins around the world.

The current GAP 24K price is tracked and recorded live at PublicGoldPrice.com, updated every 3 minutes throughout the day.

How Does Public Gold GAP 24K Work?

Think of GAP 24K as a "digital gold savings account" — but instead of earning interest (which would be haram), your savings are directly converted into gold at the prevailing market rate.

The process in simple steps:

  • You deposit money — in Malaysian Ringgit (RM)
  • Public Gold converts it to gold — at the current GAP 24K rate
  • Gold is recorded in your name — in the Public Gold system
  • Gold value moves with the market — following global gold prices
  • You can sell back anytime — to Public Gold at the prevailing rate
  • You can redeem physically — as gold bars, coins, or wafers

There is no fixed tenure or lock-in period. You invest on your own terms.

What is the Minimum Investment for GAP 24K?

There is no fixed minimum investment amount for GAP 24K. You can buy any amount of gold based on the current price per gram. Most investors start with somewhere between RM100 to RM500 as their first purchase and build their holdings gradually over time.

GAP 24K vs SAP 999 — What is the Difference?

Public Gold offers two accumulation programs: GAP 24K for gold and SAP 999 for silver. Here is a quick comparison:

  • GAP 24K — 24-karat pure gold, priced per gram, more stable value
  • SAP 999 — 999 pure silver, priced per 100 grams, more price volatile but lower entry cost

Many investors hold both as a diversification strategy. You can track both prices live at PublicGoldPrice.com (gold) and PublicGoldPrice.com (silver).
