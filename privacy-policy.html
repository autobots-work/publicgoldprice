Effective: 5 March 2026

PublicGoldPrice.com does not require you to register or provide any personal information to use our gold and silver price tracker. However, we may automatically collect the following information:

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand visitor behaviour on our website. GA4 collects data such as pages visited, session duration, traffic sources, and device information. This data is processed by Google and we only receive aggregated reports.

This website may display advertisements through Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-5267120092715349). Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites.
