Public Gold vs HelloGold 2026 — Which is Better for Malaysian Investors?

Quick Answer

Public Gold and HelloGold are two of Malaysia's most popular gold investment platforms, but they are fundamentally different. Public Gold offers physical 24-karat gold (GAP 24K) with comprehensive Shariah certification from Amanie Advisors, while HelloGold offers digital gold backed by physical gold stored in a vault. The best choice depends on whether you prioritise physical ownership or digital convenience.

Choosing between Public Gold and HelloGold is one of the most common questions among Malaysian gold investors today. Both platforms are popular, both claim Shariah compliance, and both offer accessible gold investment — but they work very differently.

In this comparison, we will examine both platforms across the key factors that matter most to Malaysian investors: safety, Shariah compliance, pricing, usability, and who each platform is best suited for.

Platform Overview

Before comparing, here is a quick snapshot of each platform:

Public Gold

  • Founded in 2008, headquartered in Johor Bahru
  • Physical 24-karat gold (GAP 24K) and 999 pure silver (SAP 999)
  • Over 350,000 active members
  • World's first Shariah certification for gold and silver — Amanie Advisors Sdn. Bhd.
  • Gold redeemable as physical bars, coins, or wafers
  • Dealer model — purchase via website or authorised dealers

HelloGold

  • Founded in 2015, headquartered in Kuala Lumpur
  • Digital gold — blockchain-based technology
  • Partnership with World Gold Council
  • Claims Shariah compliance based on AAOIFI standards
  • Gold backed by physical gold stored in a vault
  • Full mobile app experience

Comparison 1 — Gold Ownership

This is the most critical difference between the two platforms.

Public Gold: You buy and own real physical gold. GAP 24K gold is fully redeemable as physical bars, coins, or wafers that you can hold in your hand, store at home, or keep in a bank safe deposit box. Ownership is clear and unambiguous.

HelloGold: You own "digital gold" backed by physical gold stored in a third-party vault. You do not hold the gold physically yourself, though you can redeem above certain thresholds. This model is closer to a "gold certificate" than direct physical ownership.

Winner: Depends on your preference. If you want physical gold you can hold — Public Gold wins decisively. If you are comfortable with digital gold records — HelloGold offers greater digital convenience.

Comparison 2 — Shariah Compliance

Public Gold: Comprehensive Shariah certification from Amanie Advisors Sdn. Bhd. — covering the entire business model including GAP 24K purchases, EPP, undertaking buyback, and the GSAP program. Public Gold is the world's first gold and silver company to receive this certification.

HelloGold: Claims Shariah compliance based on AAOIFI standards. However, digital gold using blockchain technology remains a topic of debate among Islamic scholars, particularly regarding the immediacy of possession (qabd) in Islamic transactions.

Winner: Public Gold, for clearer and more comprehensive Shariah credentials that are more widely accepted by local Islamic authorities.

Comparison 3 — Price Transparency

Public Gold: Buy and sell prices are published openly and updated multiple times daily. You can track every price change at PublicGoldPrice.com — every update is timestamped and recorded.

HelloGold: Prices are based on global gold rates with their own spread applied. Pricing is available in the app but not as granularly tracked as Public Gold's daily price records.

Winner: Public Gold, for the most transparent price tracking available in the Malaysian market.

Comparison 4 — Usability

Public Gold: Available via website, mobile app, and a wide network of authorised dealers across Malaysia for face-to-face service. Ideal for investors who value in-person support.

HelloGold: Sleek, fully digital mobile app experience. Easier for tech-savvy investors who prefer handling everything on their phone.

Winner: HelloGold for pure digital convenience. Public Gold for dealer support and physical redemption options.

Final Verdict — Which Should You Choose?

There is no single "better" platform — it depends on what matters most to you:

  • Choose Public Gold if: you want physical gold you can redeem, the strongest Shariah credentials, price transparency, and face-to-face dealer support
  • Choose HelloGold if: you want a fully digital experience and are comfortable with digital gold records
  • Consider both: many investors hold both platforms as a diversification strategy

For investors who prioritise physical ownership, Shariah clarity, and price transparency — Public Gold is the stronger choice among Malaysian gold investment platforms.
